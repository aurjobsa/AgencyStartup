Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you provide candidate profiles?

We typically share the first batch of pre-screened candidate profiles within 48-72 hours of receiving your detailed requirements. For urgent roles, we can expedite this to 24-48 hours.

What if we're not satisfied with the candidates?

We offer unlimited candidate sourcing until you find the right fit. If you're not satisfied with our initial profiles, we'll refine our search based on your feedback and provide better-matched candidates.

Do you handle remote hiring across different time zones?

Yes, we specialize in remote hiring and can coordinate interviews across different time zones. Our team is experienced in managing global remote hiring processes efficiently.

What's included in your employer branding service?

Our employer branding includes LinkedIn company page optimization, career page development, talent attraction content strategy, and building your reputation as an employer of choice in your industry.

Do you provide replacement guarantee?

Yes, we provide a 90-day replacement guarantee. If a hired candidate doesn't work out within the first 90 days, we'll source and provide a replacement at no additional cost.
